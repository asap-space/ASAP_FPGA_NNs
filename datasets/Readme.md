# NASA Magnetosperic MultiScale (MMS) mission dataset
The data within these datasets is obtained from the Fast Plasma Investigation (FPI) device onboard the four Magnetospheric Multiscale (MMS) spacecraft. This data can be downloaded from https://lasp.colorado.edu/mms/sdc/public/datasets/fpi/.

Olshevsky et al. [1] labeled the MMS data from November 2017 and December 2017 as one of four categories:
-1. Undefined/Unknown
0. Solar Wind (SW)
1. Ion foreshock (IF)
2. Magnetosheath (MSH)
3. Magnetosphere (MSP)

[1] Olshevsky, V., Khotyaintsev, Y. V., Lalti, A., Divin, A., Delzanno, G. L., Anderzén, S., et al. (2021). Automated classification of plasma regions using 3D particle energy distributions. Journal of Geophysical Research: Space Physics, 126, e2021JA029620. https://doi.org/10.1029/2021JA029620
